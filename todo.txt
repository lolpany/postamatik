--- postamatik concepts -------------------------------

top level
    Content - Picture/Audio/Video
    ContentTagger - tags content with Tags
    TaggedContent - Content with Tags

    Tag - just word to categorize (same as, for example, in youtube)

    Locations - Group/Page/Channel
    LocationTagger - tags location with Tags
    TaggedLocation - Location with Tags

    Solver - gets locations, matches content and pushes Post (with assigned time) into scheduler
    Scheduler - checks for Posts with time and posts them, simplest possible, general as possible
    ContentStreamer - copies content from Source to Location
